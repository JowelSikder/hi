Overview

At the verge of new millennium Life Science School was introduced in SUST, under which department of Genetic Engineering and Biotechnology (GEB) was the first to start. After its inception in 2004, this department rose rapidly as a center of excellence in life science. This department offers dynamic field of study and research, which will help to prepare a student to become one of tomorrow's leaders. Our vibrant undergraduate and postgraduate programs provide a broad knowledge of theoretical and empirical aspects of biotechnology.
